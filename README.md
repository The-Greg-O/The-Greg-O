## Greg Overton

**Head of Research Engineering at [Butlr](https://butlr.com)** · Seattle, WA

I turn complex systems into things that actually work in the real world: edge
hardware, ML, security, and the product wrapped around them. About 13 years of
it, across aerospace, security, and IoT. I started in Air Force cyberspace and
space ops, moved into solutions engineering in security at Rapid7 and Palo Alto
Networks, and landed at Butlr, the privacy-first occupancy platform, where I
oversee mega deployments and lead our Research Engineering function.

Right now my focus is edge and ML infrastructure, thermal sensing and computer
vision, fleet-scale systems, and the AI-native tooling we use to build and
operate all of it.
